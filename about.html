About us

Jordan Game Awards first launced back in May of 2010 with a goal to provide a community for gamers to share their interests, opinions and fondness of games.

Within months; JGA had already started their own "Academy Awards" and it quickly took off. Gamers and journalists from all over the world came to write, vote and share the idea of an awards show for games!

In 2015, Jordan Game Awards was critical for any games success. If a game was on the top charts of JGA; it was on the top charts in sales!

Jordan Game Awards is in no way affiliated with The Game Awards; this is strictly for educational purposes and Jordan Game Awards receives no benefits.
